Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 21, 2024

MediAssist AI is committed to transparency. We do not sell your data, and we do not use your medical information for advertising or profile-building.

1. Data We Collect

Personal Identification Information

We collect your email address and name via Apple or Google Authentication. This is used solely for account identification and subscription management.

Health & Consultation Data

Inputs you provide (symptoms, history, images) are processed in real-time. We use Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure that only you can access your personal records.

Real-Time Communications

For video/audio features, we use Agora. MediAssist AI does not record or store your live audio/video streams unless you explicitly trigger a "Save Session" feature.

2. AI Data Processing Disclosure

MediAssist AI utilizes advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) from OpenAI (GPT-4), Google (Gemini), and Anthropic (Claude). Data sent to these providers is:

  • Sent over encrypted TLS connections.
  • Anonymized where possible.
  • Not used by these providers to train their global models (Enterprise-grade API usage).

3. Payment Security

Payment processing is handled via Stripe. MediAssist AI never sees or stores your full credit card number or CVV. Stripe provides us only with a secure token to manage your subscription.

4. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To delete your account: Open the App -> Settings -> Delete Account. This will immediately and permanently scrub all your data from our Supabase servers.

5. Security Standards

We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption for data at rest and SSL/TLS for data in transit. Our infrastructure is hosted on AWS and Supabase, adhering to SOC2 compliance standards.
